About Me

I'm Ibrahim Eltamawy, a Senior Backend Engineer with expertise in .NET/ASP.NET Core development. Currently at Traveler Club, I work on large-scale travel and booking systems serving ~200K users, specializing in backend architecture, API design, and system integration.

With a strong focus on system architecture and technical leadership, I've mentored teams of up to 11 engineers and contributed to modernizing legacy systems into robust .NET Core solutions. I'm passionate about building reliable, high-performance systems and sharing knowledge with teams.
